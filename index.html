Awanomy is working with Pinpoint Irrigation Co. as its first strategic business partner to explore deployment of adaptive, distributed intelligence directly at irrigation endpoints. Status: Early strategic collaboration

This partnership supports validation of Awanomy's hardware-level intelligence platform in real-world irrigation environments where centralized sensing networks are costly or impractical.
